Digital Transformation

Digital transformation is reimagining of business models into the digital age and customer experiences to meet changing business and market requirements.

Process Transformation: A significant focus of corporate activity has been in business processes. Cloud, APIs, analytics, data, AI, machine learning and other technologies offer customers valuable new ways to help throughout the customers/clients the goal of cost optimization, reducing development times, faster to market with increased quality.

Business Model Transformation: Companies are pursuing digital technologies to transform traditional business models to Cloud based business models. Whereas process transformation focuses on current business, The Goal of business model transformations is a fundamental building blocks of how we deliver the value in the industry.

Domain Transformation: New technologies like Cloud, Big-Data and AI are helping customers to redefine products and services, blurring industry boundaries and creating entirely new sets of opportunity and end user experience with social good.

Cultural/Organizational Transformation: Requires redefine the organizational with growth mindsets, digitalized processes, Training the employees, and develop talent & capabilities for the digital world. Identify the change in corporations recognize digital requires agile way, a bias toward testing and learning, decentralized decision-making, and a greater reliance on the company world.

Competencies vs Skills

A competency is more than just knowledge and skills. It involves the ability to meet complex demands in a particular context.

Skills are specific to task, while competencies incorporate a set of skills with abilities and knowledge. Skills are just one of three facets that make up a competency; the other two are knowledge and abilities.
