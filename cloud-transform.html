Cloud Transformation

Cloud transformation services helps to the enterprises migrating a company's current IT infrastructure to the cloud with digital and analytics that drive innovation.

Cloud Adaption: Stages of Cloud Adoption is perform the gap Analysis and get moving towards migration and create a plan for Cloud Migration Strategy and start implementing the Migration steps and perform the migration. Once migration completed and cost optimization would be performed with the Cloud environment and Cloud adoption journey would involves types of cloud deployment models and Each deployment model is planned according to what kind of infrastructure is best-in-class for that business in the cost effective manners. Cloud Adaption would based on cloud service models like Software as a Service, Platform as a Service, and Infrastructure as a Service.

Re-engineering: Re-Engineering is the act of recreating a current legacy core business process with the goal of improving product features, quality, reducing costs and High class customer experience with digitalized way. Typically, it involves the understanding legacy/existing business implementations, finding place for improvements that are below-the-par or inefficient, and solve using best suitable technologies or change them in the reduced operation cost.

Cost Optimization: Cloud cost optimization is the process of reducing operation cost and overall cloud spend by identifying un-utilized resources, mismanaged resources, identify resource can be shared across teams, reserving capacity for higher discounts, and Right Sizing of computing services to scale. Can use best cloud monitoring products to help find improvement areas and security gaps.

Competencies vs Skills

A competency is more than just knowledge and skills. It involves the ability to meet complex demands in a particular context.

Skills are specific to task, while competencies incorporate a set of skills with abilities and knowledge. Skills are just one of three facets that make up a competency; the other two are knowledge and abilities.
